About Henry

I am a fourth‑year electrical engineering student at UC Santa Barbara with hands‑on experience in integrated circuit design, power electronics, and photonics. As a Senior Student Technician, I've led technical projects, supervised over 10 employees, and coordinated over 300 lecture spaces while improving troubleshooting efficiency by 50%.

My research experience includes work on inverse design of diffractive optical elements and metasurfaces, where I optimized MATLAB models based on electromagnetic theory. I've presented findings to cohorts of 50+ researchers and led weekly research meetings. My current projects focus on switched‑capacitor DC‑DC converters, achieving 92% efficiency in power conversion.

I value rigorous design methodology, clear technical communication, and collaborative problem‑solving. My experience spans from semiconductor fabrication to power electronics, with proficiency in MATLAB, LTSpice, KiCAD, and various hardware tools.
